Black Tea

A short trivia about black tea - its background, characteristics, and variations.

Background

The most popular type of tea and known for 85% of total tea consumption in Western culture, black tea is a fully-oxidized tea dried for preparation. This situation provides a darker appearance and stronger flavor than other tea varieties.

Due to its fully-oxidized characteristics, the black tea has higher caffeine content compared to other teas - around half of the level of coffee, and its appearance ranges from dark amber to black-colored infusion.
