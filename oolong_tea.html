Oolong Tea

A short trivia about oolong tea - its background, characteristics, and variations.

Background

Also known as Wulong tea, oolong tea is a semi-oxidized tea, and its colored infusion ranges between green and black tea, depending on its preparation. The longer oxidation results in a darker, amber-colored oolong tea (like black tea) - while shorter oxidation results in a lighter, greenish-yellow oolong tea (like green tea).

Due to its varying oxidation levels from the different preparation methods, oolong tea has less caffeine than black tea, but has more caffeine than green tea.
