White Tea

A short trivia about white tea - its background, characteristics, and variations.

Background

Originally from Fujian, China, white tea is the least processed of all teas due to its underdeveloped nature from being harvested from the unopened buds and young leaves of tea plants.

Due to its underdeveloped nature, this tea produces a very mild flavor and light-colored infusion. It also tends to have less caffeine than other tea varieties and has a very high level of oxidants.

3 Popular Types of White Tea

Yin Zhen Bai Hao
(Silver Needle)

Bai Mu Dan
(White Peony)

Gongmei
(Tribute Eyebrow)
