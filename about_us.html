About Us

The story behind TFTWT and its goal, mission, and vision.

What is TFTWT All About?

Our Story

The FanTEAstic World of Tea is a passion project created by Monique Sy for MINTERO in DLS-CSB - under the guidance of Sir Melvin Moso. The website aims to provide a simple crash course on the basic tea categories and their different variations while providing a concise history of the origins and influences of tea and a few tea recommendations.

What We Do

TFTWT aims to introduce the beauty of tea to people through its history, varieties, usages, and recommendations while making it enjoyable through its visuals and concise information.

Mission

TFTWT inspires people to learn and appreciate the beauty of tea and its rich background, wide varieties, different utilizations, and ability to cater to different kinds of people.

Vision

TFTWT's goal is to spread awareness on the impact of tea in forming our identities due to its influence on the cultures and lifestyles of the people.
