Green Tea

A short trivia about green tea - its background, characteristics, and variations.

Background

One of the most popular teas worldwide, green tea is an unoxidized tea due to it being heated immediately after being harvested from the plant to destroy the enzymes that cause oxidation.

This practice causes the green tea to preserve a high level of antioxidants, vitamins, and minerals for the health benefits of green tea - and causes the tea to produce a greenish to yellow-colored infusion and has less caffeine than black tea.
